# Roadmap — template

> **How to use this file.** A roadmap is a communication tool, not a promise. Fill in the vision and the **now / next / later** columns; use dates only where you'd stake money on them. Re-order ruthlessly — the point is to say what you're _not_ doing as clearly as what you are. Keep the "Won't do" section honest; it's the most useful part for both your team and your AI agents.
